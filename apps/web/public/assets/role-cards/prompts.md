# Werewolf Role Card Asset Prompts

These prompts define the production role-card asset direction. Keep future role cards in the same deck style.

## Shared Style

Premium illustrated board game card art, painterly but crisp, sophisticated dark fantasy, moonlit Werewolf browser-game deck. Use vertical 2:3 card composition, parchment-gold borders, deep teal-black shadows, readable character silhouettes, and no readable text.

## Card Back

Generate one finished hidden identity card back, 2:3 vertical card art, no text. Ornate symmetrical card back with crescent moon, subtle wolf-eye motif hidden in linework, circular rune geometry, parchment-gold border, deep teal-black field, soft moon glow, restrained teal rim light.

## Villager

Grounded human villager portrait, waist-up, warm eyes, simple cloak and linen clothing, dignified and trustworthy, ordinary person with quiet courage, no obvious tools.

## Werewolf

Shadowed human-wolf hybrid portrait, waist-up, intelligent predatory eyes, torn dark cloak, restrained menace, mostly humanoid silhouette with wolf traits.

## Witch

Human witch/apothecary mystic portrait, waist-up, calm sharp expression, layered dark cloak, subtle potion glow near hands but face remains main subject, elegant and dangerous.

## Seer

Hooded human oracle/seer portrait, waist-up, thoughtful eyes, subtle moonlit veil, faint celestial glow around eyes, wise and restrained.

## Guard

Human village guard/sentinel portrait, waist-up, protective posture, simple leather-and-cloth armor, calm vigilance, shield motif secondary in background only.
